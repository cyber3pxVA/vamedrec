📊 How the 2-Stage Pipeline Works

🔬 Stage 1: NLP Extraction

  • Extracts medication names
  • Identifies doses & frequencies
  • Detects negation (stopped/discontinued)
  • Finds temporal information
  • 100% deterministic (no AI guessing)

🤖 Stage 2: AI Reconciliation

  • Compares prior vs current medications
  • Identifies matches & discrepancies
  • Detects dose changes
  • Finds new additions & discontinuations
  • Flags ambiguities for review
